About Word Counter App
What is Word Counter?

The Word Counter App is a simple yet effective tool designed to count the number of words in any text inputted by the user. This tool is helpful for writers, students, content creators, and anyone who needs to quickly analyze the length of their text.

Key Features:
  • Count the total number of words in a sentence.
  • Display the list of individual words in the sentence.
  • Simple, user-friendly interface for easy access.
  • Fast and efficient word count analysis.
How it Works:

Simply enter a sentence into the text box, and the app will automatically count the number of words and display each word individually along with the word count.
